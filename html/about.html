Who We Are

The IEEE Student Branch at Amity University Patna is a vibrant, student-led organization affiliated with the IEEE Kolkata Section. Our mission is to foster technical growth, leadership, and collaboration among students. We provide a platform to explore innovation, attend events, and become part of a global community.

Our Vision

To inspire students to lead technological innovation that serves humanity, promoting collaboration and ethics in engineering and science.

Our Mission

To create a platform for students to develop technical skills, contribute to projects, and connect with IEEE professionals globally.

Event Highlight

IEEE Student Branch Inauguration at Amity University Patna

About the Event

On 10th July 2025, Amity University Patna proudly inaugurated its official IEEE Student Branch under the IEEE Kolkata Section. The event marked a significant milestone in promoting research, innovation, and professional development among students.

The inauguration was graced by eminent IEEE members, academic leaders, and student participants. Various sessions on IEEE benefits, technical talks, and leadership responsibilities were held. The enthusiastic participation from students and faculty set the stage for many future IEEE activities.

The branch aims to foster a spirit of technological excellence and global collaboration through events, workshops, and community-driven projects.
